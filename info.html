Everett Gold (2008, Los Angeles) is a photographer and creative director that resides in LA and NYC. Starting off with photography but expanding into visual practices and directing, his practice fluctuates between documentary and narrative construction. Shaped through exchanges with various collaborators, his practice leans into the unexpected while focusing on authentic expression through music, fashion, and today’s cultural landscape. The work frequently balances themes of duality, transparency and contradiction that suggests rather than confirming meaning.
